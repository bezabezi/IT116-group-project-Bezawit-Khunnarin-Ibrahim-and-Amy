About Us

Welcome to CABO restaurant, where we invite you to experience our vibrant flavors and deliciously rich authentic Mexican dishes.Nestled in the heart of Cabo San Lucas, our restaurant offers an authentic taste of Mexico in every bite.
We take pride in offering all of our guests an exceptional dining experience, where every dish is crafted with love and care. From cheese melting Carne Asada quesadilla's to savory taco al pastor dipped in a flavorful and juicy consume, our menu features a wide array of traditional Mexican dishes, each bursting with flavor and authenticity.
